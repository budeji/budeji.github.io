13. 健谈的人

When it comes to chatterboxes, I immediately think of the owner of a flower shop. The shop opened up near my home last year. It’s owned by an elderly woman who is in her sixties. Even though she’s older, she can talk your ear off, and I think that’s a big reason why her shop is doing well.

No matter who walks into her shop, she can strike up a conversation with them. It’s like she’s got a

never-ending supply of things to say. For example, the owner is a huge gardening enthusiast. To make the best use of those flowers and increase her income, she decided to teach flower arranging classes. Many people followed her to learn floral techniques and practice flower arrangement. What’s impressive is how she can talk non-stop in her classes. She can go into all the nitty-gritty details, and even after the class is done, she can just keep the conversation going, like talking about how physically demanding it is to arrange bouquets every day

Plus, the owner created a WeChat group where customers can ask any questions they may have about purchasing flowers or flower arranging. She often sends tons of voice messages to customers, each lasting for about 60 seconds, and she talks without taking many breaks. I truly respect her for having such a wealth of thoughts and ideas.

Anyways, her talkative nature is never bothersome. Instead, everyone finds her super sincere and enthusiastic. I especially admire her for that, and I believe her business will continue to thrive.

14. 好客的人

I’d like to talk about my mom’s friend, who is in her fifties and is just amazing at hosting guests.

Whenever someone visits her, she’s the kind of person who insists on going to the kitchen and whipping up something special. She lives on the ground floor and has a beautiful garden where she grows different vegetables and fruits. When guests are at her house, she always takes them to her little garden, lets them pick out the veggies they want, and then creates delicious meals for them.

Plus, when the weather is nice, she loves having friends and relatives over in her yard for barbecues

and parties. She personally takes charge of all the barbecue preparations. She even remembers everyone’s favorites, like who’s into garlic-grilled shrimp and who’s all about the sweet-flavored grilled shrimp. So, you don’t have to worry about being left out. She’s always there, making you feel super comfy and at home.

Anyways, her home always feels lively, and she often has lots of visits from friends and family. I think she’s an excellent host because she enjoys socializing. Besides, my mom said her friend honed her cooking skills at a very young age. Back then, her parents had to work away from home to make ends meet, so she had to learn to feed herself. She also worked in a school cafeteria for many years, which gaveher valuable experience.

So, for someone who can cook and loves cooking for others, it’s no surprise sheleaves a warm impression on people.
